The best Movies & Series according to Kyle

My top Movies & series of all-time.

Peaky Blinders

This is probably my favorite show of all time, the time it's set in & how the characters play their roles is 10/10.
The only thing i wish is that there were more seasons.

Game of Thrones

I'd say this is tied with the number one spot, everything about this series is next level.
The battles, the history between houses & the story with the white walkers make this series one of the best of all time for me.

The Fast and the Furious

The OG fast and Furious movie has to be up there was one of the greatest movies of all time.
Granted the sequals to this movie have progressivley gotten worse but this movie itself stands out for me from my childhood.
Amazing cars and great race scenes, what it should've stayed like!
